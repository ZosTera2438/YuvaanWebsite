Ek Sunhera
Karvaan

The Literature and Film Council (LFC) is one of the largest cultural councils in Delhi Technological University. It is the place to be for our college's bookwarms and movie buffs. We are a family of 600+ members working and in different fields as per their interest. DTU LFC has been organizing it's flagship event, the literature and film festival, "Yuvaan" annually for the last six years running with immense success. Any council develops by collective efforts of it's members and we can proudly say that our council has come very far ahead from where we started. We have collaborated with hundreds of brands and artists every year to conduct many amazing events such as celebrity meet-ups, movie screenings, book launches and of course, the crowd favourite "Bollywood Night." This year is going to be no different because DTU LFC is aiming for the stars and host a ground breaking Yuvaan 2023 - "Ek Sunehra Karwan."
